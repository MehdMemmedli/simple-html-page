The player who has shattered almost every record, for many, the best player in history. He arrived at Barça at the age of 13 and the club paid for his growth hormone treatment. Top scorer in the history of FC Barcelona; Foreign player with more matches in the history of the club; Top scorer in league history; Maximum assistant in the history of the League; World's top scorer in a calendar year (91 goals in 2012); Top scorer in a league (50 goals in 2011-12); Author of the goal 5,000 (01/02/2009) and 6000 (18/08/2018) of FC Barcelona in the League; Author of FC Barcelona’s 500th goal in the European Cup / Champions (30/09/2014). First player to score 100 goals in international competitions at the same club. Only player to score more than 30 official goals in 12 consecutive seasons (2008-2020). Player with more consecutive matches (21) scoring at least one goal (33 goals) in the First Division (2012-13). Player who has been the top scorer (7) in a major league in the world. Only player in history to win 3 consecutive golden boots. Youngest player (23 years) in the history of football to win 15 club titles. Only player to get 4 consecutive gold balls (2009-10-11-12). World's top scorer in a single club; Top non-European goal scorer in the history of the Champions League; Maximum assistant in the history of the Champions. Nicknamed ‘La Pulga’, his goal at Getafe at the Camp Nou (18/04/2007), is considered one of the best in history and he scored in two Champions League finals (2008-09 and 2010-11). In 2007 he promoted the Leo Messi Foundation, which aims to help the most needy children in Argentina
